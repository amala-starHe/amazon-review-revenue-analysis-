
# 📊 From Reviews to Revenue: Leveraging Amazon Feedback for Business Growth

## 🧠 Problem Statement
Customer reviews contain valuable insights about product performance, customer satisfaction, and improvement opportunities.  
This project analyzes Amazon product reviews (Stanley Cup dataset) to uncover patterns that can drive **business growth and revenue optimization**.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- SQL (for structured querying)
- Tableau (for visualization)
- Excel (data preprocessing)

---

## 🔍 Key Steps
- Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
- Sentiment analysis on customer reviews  
- Identifying trends in ratings and feedback  
- Visualization of key KPIs  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Identified major drivers of positive and negative customer sentiment  
- Detected recurring product issues affecting ratings  
- Found correlations between review sentiment and customer satisfaction  

---

## 💼 Business Impact
- Suggested product improvements to enhance customer satisfaction  
- Identified opportunities for pricing and marketing optimization  
- Demonstrated how customer feedback can be leveraged to drive revenue
